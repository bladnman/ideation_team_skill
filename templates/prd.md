## Why This Document Exists

_One paragraph: this PRD translates a multi-agent ideation session into a set of requirements that another agent or team can use to plan and execute implementation. It preserves the intent, reasoning, and emotional logic of the ideation process. The focus is on what should be built and why — not how to build it. Technical details from the ideation session are preserved as context, not as implementation directives._

## What We're Asking For

_A narrative description of the product direction. What does this look like when it's working? What experience is being created? Write this as if describing the product's identity to someone who will figure out how to build it. Not a feature list — a picture of what the product is trying to be._
